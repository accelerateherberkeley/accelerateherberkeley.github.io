Our Organization

AccelerateHer @ Berkeley is a student organization that brings together a diverse team of femxle and non-binary identifying individuals in a creative space to grow as a robust entrepreneur in business. By joining one of our three organizing committees, you will have the opportunity to chat with industry trailblazers on how they are changing their fields, work with Berkeley SkyDeck to facilitate a program that fosters innovative business solutions through our AccelerateHer Innovation Fellowship, and visit dozens of high-impact unicorn ventures located right here in the Bay Area.

Our organizing committees are searching for future team members in four concentrations:

  • Innovation
  • Finance
  • Marketing & Communications
  • Professional Development

Our Committees

Innovation

As an integral team member of our Innovation committee, you will work alongside our Executive team to provide resources and organize curriculum for our Innovation Fellowship and startup founders. In the latter portion of the Spring semester, members will have a key role in organizing our annual AccelerateHer Startup Weekends!

Finance

Our Finance committee works as the glue to our organization. As a committee member you will be working with our executives leading Finance and Marketing to create marketing and budgeting strategies for the organization year round. As a part of the finance committee, members will lead task forces on acquiring and managing our funds and bank accounts, as well as prepare budgets for the AccelerateHer Startup Weekends and Innovation Fellowship key dates.

Marketing and Communications

As a marketing & communications committee member, you will lead innovative marketing strategies throughout the year for our main social platforms as well as content development for our website. We are currently in search of team members who have a passion for photography/videography, graphic design, content marketing, communications and/or web development!

Professional Development

We at AxH believe that people are the center of our organization. If you think so too, this is the committee for you! The Professional Development committee focuses on people operations, professional development and community building amongst the members of AccelerateHer. This committee will focus on creating opportunities for the organization members to get to know like-minded students and professionals through socials and coffee chates within our organization, collaboration-events with other organizations and company visits to dozens of high impact startups in the Bay Area. The PD committee will also organize two professional development workshops every year as a team that are open to all students of Berkeley!
