Who Are We?

At AccelerateHer, we collaborate with change-driven femxles to accelerate entrepreneurial ideas and consult growth-stage companies in order to strengthen key skills and influence of femxle leaders in the business world.

Sponsored by

Entrepreneurship

Supported by experienced accelerators and startup founders, we host annual startup weekends and fellowship programs to nurture budding ideas into a concrete business opportunity. Driven to develop problem-solvers and leaders of tomorrow, our startup weekends are aimed towards fostering a community of femxle entrepreneurs, consultants and industry experts.

Consulting

With a diverse set of expertise and interests, our analysts undergo a comprehensive training program and rigorous startup weekends to strategically develop solutions tailored to the needs of the growth-stage companies located in the San Francisco Bay Area

Fostering a community of shared ideas through collective innovation and cooperation

Our Focus

At WEB, our aim is to develop female identifying individuals into entrepreneurs who are effective risk-takers and holistic thinkers in their respective fields of interest. Instead of restricting the scope of entrepreneurship to technology, we encourage entrepreneurship across eight diverse fields.
