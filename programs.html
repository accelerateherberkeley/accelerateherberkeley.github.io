What We Do

AccelerateHer at Berkeley presents to you Cal's first ever AccelerateHer Startup Weekends co-hosted by Berkeley Haas Entrepreneurship and powered by Blackstone LaunchPad and National Science Foundation (NSF)!

Build a startup over two weekends with professional support and pitch it to industry leaders to unlock your future entrepreneurial success. Over the two weekends, you collaborate with your team members to ideate, discover customers, build prototypes, validate business opportunity and pitch your startup.

And while you build a startup you have a workspace for the weekends, 10 interactive startup workshops, mentorship from top startup founders, networking mixers, startup toolkits and much more. Eligibility

Any berkeley student with a bright idea!

Team Regulations
  • Teams must consist of at least 3 and at most 5 members. If you don't have a team, don't worry! We will help you find one
  • Every team member must apply individually
  • Teams must have at least 2 female identifying members
  • Every team member must be present on the first and last day of the weekends
  • At least half the team must be available to attend the entire program
