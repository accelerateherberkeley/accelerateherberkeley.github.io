Our Organization & Mission

Women Entrepreneurs @ Berkeley is a startup development student-run organization based in University of California, Berkeley. We strive to create a network where entrepreneurship in a variety of disciplines as well as across disciplines is encouraged and supported for individuals identifying as females. Our innovative entrepreneurial culture is based on four core vaues

Empowerment

Providing young women with the tools to find their voice and fearlessly pursue their passions

Mentorship

Facilitating the sharing and teaching skills by cultivating a synergetic learning environment

Collaboration

Fostering a community of shared ideas through collective innovation and cooperation

Resourcefulness

Improving young women's access to opportune resources (financial, human, material or social)



Our Focus

At WEB, our aim is to develop female identifying individuals into entrepreneurs who are effective risk-takers and holistic thinkers in their respective fields of interest. Instead of restricting the scope of entrepreneurship to technology, we encourage entrepreneurship across eight diverse fields.
Art
Media Production
Fashion
Food
Natural Science
Philanthrophy
Sports
Technology
