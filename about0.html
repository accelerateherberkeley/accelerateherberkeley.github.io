Recruitment Process

Our recruitment process for Spring 2019 will kick-off with info sessions and a social during early February. These events aim to provide an in-depth understanding of our organization experience and culture to all the prospective applicants. Following these would be coffee chats which would be open by invite only.

Apply for general membership for Spring 2019 at: https://goo.gl/forms/nlFKsuVresLqjliC2





Info-Sessions

The objective of both the info-sessions is to give prospective applicants greater insights into our organization, with a specific emphasis on our values, culture, streams and spring semester curriculum for each stream. The info-sessions will also go through the significant aspects of the selection process. In case, you cannot make it to the first info-session on 5th February, you may attend the second one on 7th February. Both the sessions cover the same material.



The Social

The objective of the social is to present prospective applicants with a chance to interact with the organization members on a personal level. This doesn't only provide the applicants with a chance to understand the organization but also build one's professional network.
